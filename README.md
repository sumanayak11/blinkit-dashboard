# Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Project Title
Blinkit Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 2. Short Description / Purpose
The Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report developed to analyze overall sales performance, customer satisfaction, and outlet efficiency.

The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, product performance, outlet distribution, and rating analysis to support data-driven business decisions.

## 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- Power BI Desktop – Data visualization and report creation  
- Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations and measures  
- Data Modeling – Creating relationships between tables  
- File Format – .pbix (Power BI file), .png (dashboard preview)

## 4. Data Source
The dataset was sourced from a YouTube tutorial video description and used for learning and hands-on practice.

The dataset includes:

- Item Visibility  
- Item Weight  
- Item Type  
- Fat Content  
- Outlet Establishment Year  
- Outlet Size  
- Outlet Location  
- Outlet Type  
- Sales  
- Customer Rating  

## 5. Business Requirement
The objective of this project is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's:

- Sales Performance  
- Customer Satisfaction  
- Inventory Distribution  
- Outlet-Level Efficiency  

The analysis focuses on:

### Sales Performance Analysis
- Measure total revenue generation  
- Identify top-performing item categories  
- Compare sales contribution by fat content  
- Analyze sales trends based on outlet establishment year  

### Customer Satisfaction Analysis
- Evaluate average customer ratings  
- Analyze relationship between ratings and sales  
- Identify customer preference patterns  

### Inventory & Product Distribution
- Analyze number of items sold by category  
- Evaluate performance across fat content segments  
- Identify high revenue contributing products  

### Outlet-Level Performance
- Compare sales across outlet sizes (Small, Medium, High)  
- Analyze geographic distribution of sales  
- Evaluate performance across outlet types  
- Study impact of outlet age on sales  

### Business Optimization Goals
- Identify high-performing segments to scale  
- Detect low-performing areas for improvement  
- Support data-driven business decisions  
- Improve operational efficiency  

## 6. KPI Requirements
The following KPIs were created:

- Total Sales – Overall revenue generated  
- Average Sales – Average revenue per sale  
- Number of Items Sold – Total quantity sold  
- Average Rating – Customer satisfaction score

## 8. Key Insights

### Sales Performance Analysis
- Blinkit generated **$1.20M in total sales** with an average sale value of **$141**
- Fruits & Snack Foods are the top performing categories, each generating **$0.18M**
- Regular fat products significantly outsell low fat items — **$776.32K vs $425.36K**
- Sales peaked in **2018 at ~$205K** and have since plateaued around **$131K**, indicating a growth slowdown

### Customer Satisfaction Analysis
- Average customer rating stands at **3.9/5**, reflecting moderate satisfaction levels
- Supermarket Type1 maintains a consistent rating of **4**, suggesting a better overall customer experience
- Improving service quality and product availability could drive higher ratings and repeat purchases

### Inventory & Product Distribution
- A total of **8,523 items** are distributed across all categories and outlets
- Fruits, Snack Foods, and Household items are the highest contributing product segments
- Low fat products have lower market representation compared to regular fat items, highlighting an untapped health-conscious segment

### Outlet Level Performance
- Tier 3 outlets lead revenue generation at **$472.13K**, outperforming Tier 2 ($393.15K) and Tier 1 ($336.40K)
- Supermarket Type1 dominates all outlet types with **$787.55K in sales** and **5,577 items sold**
- High-size outlets contribute the most revenue at **$507.90K**, confirming that outlet size directly impacts sales performance
